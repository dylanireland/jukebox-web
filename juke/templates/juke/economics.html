Jukebox

Economics

The Jukebox application charges fees when adding a song in order to keep the application exclusive and intentional. Fees are variable depending on the length of queue at the time of publishing, and the length of the song being added (measured in block intervals). Calculations are performed in USD, while payments are made in Ether. This Ether is stored in a smart contract which, upon release of Jukebox v2, will be swapped for JUK tokens, with the majority being airdropped to previous users of the platform. See Tokenomics
